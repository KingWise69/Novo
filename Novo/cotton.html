Cotton Ginning

We're a leading cotton ginning company located in Nyakesi, Uganda. Our state-of-the-art ginnery boasts a ginning capacity of 120 bales per shift, thanks to our 30 double roller gins. We pride ourselves on producing high-quality cotton lint, which we export to Europe, Asia, Malaysia, South Africa, and other countries that offer competitive rates.

Cotton Ginning

Cotton Ginning

We take quality control seriously. That’s why we have contracted Audit Control Experts (ACE) to monitor and control the quality of our lint. The government body, Cotton Development Organization (CDO), also oversees our operations and issues Lint Export Certificates annually to monitor lint export trade.

We are proud members of the Uganda Ginners and Cotton Exporters Association (UGCEA), which allows us to contribute to the planning of the sustenance of the cotton industry as a whole. Our compliance with the requirements of the Uganda Export Promotions Board ensures that we operate in line with industry standards.

Aside from our ginning hall, we have six large stores where we store seed cotton and grain. Additionally, we have annex land and plenty of open land that we plan to use for diversifying into the manufacturing of other finished goods.

About Us
