Frequently Asked Questions

A comprehensive resource designed to answer your most common queries and provide valuable insights into our products, services, and the industrial landscape.

How does your Board’s expertise drive company success and growth?

Our Board of Directors is comprised of experienced professionals who bring their expertise in finance, marketing, and human resource management to the table. Our Chairman, Mr. Mitesh R. Doshi, oversees all of our company’s activities, including delivery of goods and services, finance source management, human resource mobilization, marketing opportunity creation, and planning in general. Our Director and Secretary, Mrs. Shairoz M Doshi, supports him in these efforts.

How has Novo Enterprises Limited’s diverse product range contributed to growth?

At Novo Enterprises Limited, we have established ourselves as the leading Manufacturers Agents for hard ware – building materials and consumer goods that range from cement, iron bars, iron sheets, paint, hollow sections, sugar, detergents, plastic products, edible oil, petroleum products, rubber products, stationery, textile, beers, spirits, beverages, e.t.c. Over the years, we have gained experience in handling products from various industries, including textiles, electronics, spirits and liquor, hard ware and construction materials, beer distribution, manufactured consumer goods, imported goods, cotton production and ginning, paints, confectioneries, tyres and tubes, stationery, and telecommunication.

How has partnering with industry leaders enhanced Novo Enterprises’ market presence?

We are proud to represent some of the biggest names in the industry, including Tororo Cement Industries Ltd., Roofing (U) Ltd., Bidco Group of Companies, Uganda Breweries Limited, Sadolin Colours, Orange Telecom – Uganda, TVS Uganda, Kakira Sugar Works, Crest Tanks Limited, M.M. Integrated Steel Mills, Pramukh Steel Limited, Tembo Steel (U) Ltd, Steel & Tube Industries Ltd, Victoria Beverages Limited, Tian Tang Limited, Trye Express Limited, and Battery Masters (U) Ltd.

About Us
